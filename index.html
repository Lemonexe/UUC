Ultimate Unit Converter

Write your expression (see examples button!) into the input text field and press enter to perform the conversion, which is into basic SI by default. You can modify the units in the "target units" field to customize your output unit (e.g. Torr into mbar). Case sensitive!
If you decide to change the target units, the program will perform dimensional analysis and if it doesn't match the input, it will add necessary basic SI units to the output to make the conversion possible (e.g. Pa to W fails, correcting to W*s/m^3).
Because of floating-point arithmetics, you might encounter rounding bugs: e.g you expect 10000 but get 9999.99996 or 10000.0000004. Sorry, it's a very fundamental thing and would be difficult to circumvent...
Oh and just a quick note: the prefix "micro" is u, not μ, since it's easier to type...
Last thing: if you miss your favorite unit and would like to see it here, contact me. Or if you have an idea how to enhance the application, of course.
